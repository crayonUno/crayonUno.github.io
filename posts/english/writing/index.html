Practicing principles#

  1. find a sample article of the same type that you want to improve.
  2. translate it to Chinese version.
  3. look at the Chinese version and write it back in English.
  4. compare the two English versions, and upgrade my expression.
  5. practice in action.
  6. after a few days, change the topic and repeat the 5 steps above.
